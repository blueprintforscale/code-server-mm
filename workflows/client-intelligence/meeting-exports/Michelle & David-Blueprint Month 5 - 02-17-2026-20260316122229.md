*   **Dispatcher Hiring and Virtual Dispatch Assistance** Decision
    *   The team evaluated the idea of hiring a dispatcher to manage overflow calls and improve lead follow-up efficiency.
    *   The option of using Curry Miller’s virtual dispatcher service was discussed, including her flat fee structure and per-inspection charges.
