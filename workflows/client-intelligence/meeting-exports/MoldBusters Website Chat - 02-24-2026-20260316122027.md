*   **CRM Integration and Zapier Setup** Update
    *   Gabriella introduced a new CRM system (Market) for invoicing, estimating, and lead management, which is expected to funnel online leads into a work pipeline.
    *   The team discussed using Zapier for integrations, and Susie proposed creating support materials like videos to explain how to set up these flows.
    *   Access and permission coordination for the new CRM were identified as necessary next steps.
    
*   **Lead Attribution and Conversion Tracking** Update
    *   Martin noted that the website already used CallRail integrated via Tag Manager for lead attribution, which would be maintained and adjusted if needed.
    *   There was a consensus to ensure that the integration of new SEO elements and landing pages would not interfere with accurate conversion tracking.
    *   Aaron requested access to relevant systems to verify the technical setup, ensuring that all inbound traffic is properly attributed.
    
*   **Safeguarding Ad Campaign Performance** Decision
    *   Susie expressed concerns based on previous experiences where uncoordinated website changes resulted in decreased ad conversions.
    *   The team agreed to exercise caution by pre-reviewing and approving all changes to prevent any negative impact on ad performance.
    *   Martin suggested isolating landing pages for Google Ads, including the option to de-index certain pages to ensure they do not interfere with overall SEO efforts.
    
*   **Communication and Review Process for Website Changes** Decision
    *   Aaron proposed sending a Google Doc outlining all proposed website updates for review to ensure team alignment before implementing any changes.
    *   The process would involve swift turnaround on updates to avoid extra billable hours and maintain ad campaign success.
    *   Clear communication was emphasized as critical, with all changes needing to be pre-approved by Gabby, Susie, and Martin.
